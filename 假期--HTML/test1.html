Internet

Internet, Chinese officially translated as the Internet , also known as the Internet . It is a global network of computers that communicate with each other using a common language . Once you connect to any of its nodes, it means your computer is connected to the Internet. The Internet currently has users all over the world, with more than a few hundred million people using the Internet, and its number of users is still rising in equal levels.
